Travel Information

From Kinshasa

Flights available from Kinshasa (FIH) to Goma (GOM) via CAA, Ethiopian Airlines, or Congo Airways.

From Goma, take a boat or ferry via Lake Kivu to Bukavu. Boats leave daily at 8 AM and 1 PM.

From Overseas

Travel to Kigali from any city in the West with Rwandair, Kenya Airways, or Brussels Airlines.

From Kigali, take a local flight to Kamembe (fixed price of $100, daily at 8:00 AM).

From Kamembe, cross the border and walk to Bukavu.
